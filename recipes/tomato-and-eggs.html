Stir-Fried Tomato and Eggs

Ingredients

  • 3 eggs
  • 1 tomato, cut into small wedges
  • Green onions, to taste
  • A little oil for frying
  • A little soy sauce
  • Salt, to taste
  • Sugar, to taste (optional)

Directions

You'll need a pan or wok with a lid.

  1. Crack the eggs in a bowl. Add part of the salt and sugar. Whisk to combine.
  2. Fry the eggs in the oil. Remove the eggs.
  3. Fry the green onions for a few seconds. Add the tomatoes and cook until soft.
  4. Add the rest of the salt and sugar, plus the small amount of soy sauce, put the lid on your pan or wok, and cook for a couple of minutes.

Oven-Roasted Brussels Sprouts

Ingredients

  • 1.5 lb brussels sprouts
  • 3 tbsp olive oil
  • 3/4 tsp kosher salt
  • 1/2 tsp freshly ground black pepper

Directions

  1. Prehead oven to 400 degres F.
  2. Cut off the ends of the brussels sprouts and pull off any yellow outer leaves.
  3. Mix the brussels sprouts in a bowl with the olive oil, salt, and pepper.
  4. Put the brussels sprouts on a baking sheet and roast for 35 - 40 minutes until crisp.
